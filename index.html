Quote Calculator — Rules & Logic

1. Base Mileage & Daily Minimums

  • Long Distance Rate: The core price of the trip is calculated by multiplying Total Miles × Selected Rate (e.g., $4.50/mi).
  • Local Daily Minimum: Trips are protected against low mileage by a $1,400 per-day minimum. If the calculated mileage cost is lower than $1,400/day, the quote automatically defaults to the $1,400 daily minimum instead.

2. Free Days vs. Extra Days

  • Earning Free Days: The bus earns 1 Free Day for trips under 750 miles. After 750 miles, the bus earns an additional 0.5 Free Days for every 250 miles driven. Driver Free Days are earned strictly as 1 per 430 miles.
  • Extra Day Charges: If the Number of Trip Days exceeds the Earned Free Days, the customer is billed for the difference at $950 per Extra Day.

3. Driver Pay & Surcharges

  • Driver Pay (Included): The first driver is paid $0.60/mile. If the total trip exceeds 1,000 miles, this drops to $0.50/mile.
  • Driver Extra Days: Drivers earn 1 free day per 430 miles (calculated strictly in whole numbers). If the trip length exceeds the driver's free days, they are paid $150 per Extra Day.
  • 2nd Driver: Adding a second driver duplicates the Driver 1 pay rules and adds it as a surcharge to the Estimated Total.
